Vegan IQ is a trivia game that tests what you know about plant-based food, nutrition, and sustainability. Over 2000 questions across 8 categories, all grounded in real data from the USDA, Poore & Nemecek (2018), NIH, and FAOSTAT.

## Why We Built It

There is a lot of information out there about veganism, plant-based nutrition, and the environmental impact of food. Some of it is accurate, some of it is not. We wanted to make a game where every question is backed by verified data -- a fun way to learn real facts about what you eat and how it affects the planet.

No LLM-generated content. Every question traces back to a specific data source.

## 8 Categories

- **Nutrition**: macronutrients, vitamins, minerals, and dietary facts about plant-based foods
- **Environment**: water use, land use, greenhouse gas emissions, and ecological impact of food production
- **Ingredients**: what is and is not vegan, hidden animal products, and food labeling
- **Food History**: origins and cultural history of plant-based foods
- **Animal Welfare**: facts about farming practices and animal agriculture
- **Health**: plant-based diets and health outcomes from peer-reviewed research
- **Cooking**: techniques, substitutions, and preparation of plant-based foods
- **Culture**: veganism around the world, traditions, and cultural practices

## How It Works

Swipe right for true, left for false. Tap your answer for multiple choice questions. Sound effects and haptic feedback make every answer feel satisfying. Track your progress locally -- no account required, no data leaves your device.

## Key Features

- **2000+ curated questions** across 8 categories
- **Swipe & tap gameplay** -- intuitive and fast
- **Dark and light mode** -- play however you prefer
- **SFX & haptics** -- satisfying audio and tactile feedback
- **Share facts** -- send interesting questions to friends
- **Fully offline** -- all questions bundled in the app
- **No account required** -- open and play

## Data Integrity

All questions are generated deterministically from structured data files. The data pipeline is written in Python and pulls from:

- **USDA FoodData Central** for nutritional composition
- **Poore & Nemecek, Science (2018)** for environmental impact metrics
- **NIH** for health and dietary reference data
- **FAOSTAT** for global agricultural statistics

Questions are not written by an LLM. The pipeline generates them from verified numbers and facts, ensuring accuracy.

## Tech

- **Client**: Flutter (Dart), single codebase for Android and iOS
- **Data**: Python pipeline generating JSON question assets
- **Ads**: Google AdMob (banner, interstitial)
- **IAP**: Apple StoreKit and Google Play Billing via in_app_purchase
- **Storage**: shared_preferences for local score and preference data
